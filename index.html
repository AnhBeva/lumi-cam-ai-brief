Hardware Requirements

Outdoor-ready hardware with audio and night operation built in.

Required

  • PoE for outdoor models
  • IP67 outdoor protection
  • IR LEDs for night vision
  • White or warm supplemental light
  • Built-in microphone and speaker
  • Two-way audio and warning sound

Options / Exclusions

  • microSD card optional
  • 2K and 4K hardware options
  • No LTE/4G in this phase
  • No battery and no solar in this phase
  • Final assembly and product test at Lumi factory in Vietnam
